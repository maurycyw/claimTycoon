# Gold Mining Sim

**Status**: Idea

## Description
An isometric strategy game where you play as a character who has inherited their grandfather's land in "gold country". You start with some spare, old equipment and must mine gold to build your wealth.

## Core Loop
1.  **Mine**: Use equipment to extract gold.
2.  **Sell**: Trade gold for cash.
3.  **Upgrade**: Buy better equipment or repair old ones.
4.  **Hire**: Employ AI workers with specific skills.
5.  **Train**: Employees gain skills over time based on their tasks.

## Goal
Retire with as much wealth as possible.

## Tech Stack
-   **Engine**: Unity.
-   **Platforms**: PC & Mac (Steam).
-   **Style**: 3D Low Poly (Isometric view).
-   **AI**: Behavior Trees or GOAP (Goal Oriented Action Planning) for employee logic.
